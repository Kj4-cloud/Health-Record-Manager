About Us
At Sehat+, our mission is to make healthcare simple, accessible, and reliable for everyone. We are passionate about empowering people to take charge of their well-being with the help of technology.
Our app provides smart medicine reminders, digital health record tracking, doctor-patient connectivity, and personalized wellness tips—all in one place.
We understand that health is personal. That’s why we prioritize security and confidentiality, ensuring your data is safe and protected at all times.
With Sehat+, you’re not just downloading an app—you’re gaining a trusted companion on your journey to better health.
